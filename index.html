MISSION

Alzheimer’s disease, to many people, is akin to a myth of sorts. Something we distinctly hear about online, or in movies and TV shows. In reality, however, AD actively affects the lives of millions of people every day.
This website’s mission is to enhance your knowledge of this disease beyond its known devastating impacts. The more you know about AD, the easier it will be to detect it in yourself or in others, and the earlier it’s detected the better it can be treated.
